Making the Map

Method
The US National Institute of Health (NIH) maintains a database of clinical trials. We searched this repository, ClinicalTrials.gov, using the "Intervention" field looking for "mesenchymal stromal" or "mesenchymal strem" or "allogeneic mesenchymal" or 'autologous mesenchymal'. Results from different searches were coregistered using the url to prevent double-counting of any one trial. The trials were geolocated based on the location of the trial, this can be and often is distinct from the sponsor of the study. Many trials had multiple locations and so those appear multiple times on the map. The size of the circle pointer annotating the trial location is determined by the number of participants enrolled in the trial.
